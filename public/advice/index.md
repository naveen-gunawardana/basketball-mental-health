# Advice Library — Mentality Sports

## What This Is

A growing library of articles on every mental challenge athletes face. Written by people who have competed and been through these situations. No generic wellness content. No therapy-speak. Practical, honest, athlete-first.

Free to read. No account required.

## Topics Covered

- **Confidence** — closing the gap between practice and game performance, rebuilding after a bad game, overcoming self-doubt
- **Anxiety** — pre-competition nerves, fear of failure, pressure from coaches and parents
- **Identity** — when sport is your whole identity, what happens after an injury, losing your starting spot
- **Motivation** — burnout, losing the love for the game, staying driven through a losing season
- **Resilience** — recovering from setbacks, mental toughness, handling adversity
- **Team dynamics** — coach communication, playing time conflicts, leadership under pressure
- **Injury recovery** — the mental side of coming back, dealing with uncertainty, staying connected to the team

## Who This Is For

High school and college athletes across all sports dealing with the mental challenges that come with competing. Parents and coaches looking for resources to support athletes.

## Full Mentorship Platform

The advice library is one part of the Mentality Sports platform. Athletes can also be matched with a 1-on-1 mentor — a current or former college athlete — for ongoing, personal mental performance support.
