# Mentality Sports

## What We Do

Mentality Sports connects high school athletes with current and former college athletes for free, 1-on-1 mental performance mentorship. We focus on the mental side of sport — confidence, anxiety, identity, motivation, and resilience — the things coaches are not trained to address.

Every mentor is a real athlete who has been through the same pressures. No therapists. No scripts. No cost.

## How It Works

1. **Apply** — Tell us your sport, level, and what you're working through mentally. Takes 3 minutes.
2. **Get Matched** — We review every application and match you with a mentor whose background fits yours.
3. **Build It** — Schedule calls when it works for you. No agenda, no scripts — just honest conversation.
4. **Go Deeper** — Use the advice library, log reflections, track weekly mental goals between sessions.

## Who We Serve

**Athletes** — High school athletes ages 13–18 dealing with playing time anxiety, confidence gaps, identity struggles, pre-competition nerves, unclear team roles, motivation plateaus, or the mental side of injury recovery. All sports.

**Mentors** — Current or former college athletes who want to give back. Minimum 15 minutes per week. Volunteer. No pay.

## What's on the Platform

- 1-on-1 mentor matching (ongoing, not one session)
- Private messaging between athlete and mentor
- Reflection journal — log mental state after practices and games
- Session tracking — notes and follow-ups logged after every check-in
- Advice library — articles on confidence, anxiety, motivation, identity, resilience
- Weekly mental goals — set and track progress with your mentor

## Pricing

Free. 100% free for athletes. Mentors volunteer their time. The platform is funded through nonprofit grants.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is this free?**
Yes. Free for athletes, forever.

**Is this therapy?**
No. Peer mentorship only. Mentors are athletes, not licensed therapists. Crisis situations are referred to qualified professionals.

**How does matching work?**
Athletes apply with their sport, level, and what they're working through. We match manually — not automated.

**How often do I meet my mentor?**
Weekly check-ins are suggested. Schedule fits around school and practice.

**What sports?**
All sports. Basketball was the starting point, but the platform is for every athlete.

**What if we're not a good fit?**
Re-matching is supported. A real match matters more than sticking with a bad one.

**Can coaches or parents refer athletes?**
Yes. Sign up at mentalitysports.com or email hello@mentalitysports.com.

**What qualifies a mentor?**
Lived experience competing at the college level. They've been through the same pressures.
