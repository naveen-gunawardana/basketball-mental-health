# Get Involved — Mentality Sports

## Two Ways to Join

### For Athletes

High school athletes (ages 13–18) can apply to be matched with a 1-on-1 mentor. The process:

1. Tell us your sport, level, and what you're working through mentally — takes 3 minutes
2. We review your application and match you with a mentor whose experience fits yours
3. Start meeting on your schedule — weekly check-ins, flexible timing
4. Access private messaging, the reflection journal, weekly goal tracking, and the advice library

**Cost:** Free. Always.

Sign up at: https://mentalitysports.com/signup?role=player

### For Mentors

Current or former college athletes can apply to mentor. What's involved:

- Approximately 15 minutes per week minimum
- 1-on-1 with one athlete matched to your background
- Volunteer — no pay, no perks
- You're here because you want to give back what you wish you'd had

Apply at: https://mentalitysports.com/signup?role=mentor

## What Sports

All sports. The platform launched with basketball in San Francisco and is expanding to all sports nationwide.
