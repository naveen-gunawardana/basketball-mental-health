# About Mentality Sports

## Our Story

The best conversations athletes have about the mental side of sport don't happen in a therapist's office or a team meeting. They happen between athletes — after a loss, in a locker room, on a long drive home.

Mentality Sports was built to create those conversations at scale — without losing what makes them work. Every mentor is someone who competed at the level above, lived through the same pressures, and chose to come back and help the next generation navigate it.

No cost to athletes. No curriculum. No corporate script. Just a real relationship with someone who actually gets it.

## What We Believe

**Athlete to athlete.** Every mentor on this platform is a current or former athlete. That's not a marketing line — it's the only reason any of this works.

**Real relationships, not programs.** We don't sell a curriculum or a coaching module. We create conditions for a real relationship between two people who share something important.

**The mental game is the game.** Confidence, identity, anxiety, resilience — these aren't soft skills. They're the difference between an athlete who thrives and one who disappears.

**Give back, no strings.** Our mentors volunteer their time. No pay, no perks. They're here because someone should have been there for them — and often wasn't.
