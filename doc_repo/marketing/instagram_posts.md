# Instagram Content — Mentality Sports

Research-backed post ideas. Three buckets: athlete stories, resources, behind the platform.
Format split: Reels for reach, carousels for saves, single image for quick punches.

## Resource / Tips

**Post 7 — Carousel (8 slides)**
Hook (slide 1): "5 things to do the night before a big game that actually help your head."
Box breathing, one-sentence journal, no film after 9pm, 5-min visualization, call someone who makes you feel like yourself.
> pregame rituals aren't just for the body. your mind needs one too.
> save this for the night before your next game.
