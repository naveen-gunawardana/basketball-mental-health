# Athlete Outreach DMs

These are for recruiting mentors directly. Keep it short, reference something specific about them, and never make it feel like a pitch.

---

## 1. College Athlete Currently Competing (Posted About Mental Health)

> Hey [name], saw your post about [specific thing they shared]. That's exactly the kind of thing high school athletes need to hear and don't. I'm building a platform called Mentality Sports that pairs college athletes with high school kids going through the same stuff. No commitment required, you set your own schedule. Would you be down to be a mentor?

---

## 2. Recently Graduated Athlete

> Hey [name], congrats on finishing up at [school]. I'm building something called Mentality Sports and I think you'd be a great fit. It's a platform that connects former college athletes with high school athletes who need someone to talk to about the mental side of sport. You'd be mentoring once a week, totally on your own schedule. Would you be open to it?

---

## 3. Athlete Who Posted About Struggling (Anxiety, Benching, Injury, etc.)

> Hey, I read what you posted about [their experience]. It takes guts to share that. I'm building a platform called Mentality Sports where athletes like you mentor high school kids going through similar things. The whole idea is that the best person to talk to about that stuff is someone who's actually been through it. Would you want to be involved?

---

## 4. Athlete With a Following Who Talks About Mental Health Content

> Hey [name], love that you're open about the mental side of competing. I'm building Mentality Sports, a platform pairing college athletes with high school athletes for one-on-one mentorship around exactly that. No pressure, but would you be interested in being a mentor or even just learning more about what we're doing?

---

## 5. Cold DM (No Specific Post, Just a College Athlete)

> Hey [name], I'm building a platform called Mentality Sports that connects college athletes with high school athletes for weekly one-on-one mentorship on the mental side of sport. Confidence, anxiety, adversity, that kind of stuff. You set your own schedule and it's a way to give back to the sport. Would you be interested in being a mentor?

---

## 6. Athlete Posting General Sports Content (Highlights, Training, Grind)

> Hey [name], been watching your content for a bit. The work you're putting in is real. I'm a sophomore in high school building a platform called Mentality Sports that pairs college athletes with high school kids for one-on-one mentorship on the mental side of competing. I think your story and what you're doing would resonate with a lot of younger athletes. Would you be open to being a mentor or even just connecting about it?

---

## 7. General Sports Content Creator (Partnership Angle)

> Hey [name], love what you're putting out. I'm building a platform called Mentality Sports that connects college athletes with high school athletes going through the grind. I think there's a real overlap between your audience and what we're building. Would you be open to a quick convo about a potential collab or partnership?

---

## Notes

- Always reference something specific about them when you can. It separates you from spam.
- Don't send more than one follow-up if they don't respond.
- If they say yes, send them the link to sign up as a mentor and follow up personally once they're on the platform.
- DM on the platform where they're most active. If they post on TikTok, DM on TikTok.
