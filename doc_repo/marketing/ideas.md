# Marketing Ideas

## Podcast: Conversations with Mentors

**Concept:** Naveen hosts a podcast interviewing notable mentors — college athletes, former pros, coaches — about their experiences with the mental side of basketball. What they wish they had. What they'd tell their younger selves. How they navigated the grind.

**Why it works:**
- Builds credibility and brand for Mentality Sports
- Creates content that attracts both mentors (they want to be featured) and players (they want to hear these stories)
- Gives mentors a platform — another reason to be involved beyond just volunteering
- Generates authentic storytelling that no ad campaign can replicate
- Positions Naveen as a genuine voice in the space, not just a founder selling something

**Format ideas:**
- Short-form (20–30 min episodes) to start
- Record over Zoom, publish on Spotify/Apple Podcasts
- Clip highlights for Instagram/TikTok

**Potential first guests:**
- Mentors already in the network (coaches, former players Naveen knows personally)
- Local college athletes at USF, SFSU, SF City College
- Expand to notable names as the brand grows

**Status:** Idea stage — not yet in active development. Revisit once pilot is running and there are real mentor stories to tell.
