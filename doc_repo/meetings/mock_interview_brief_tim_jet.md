# First Meeting Guide — Mentor x Player

## The Goal

Player walks away with **one thing** to try this week — an attitude shift, a mental trick, something concrete.

---

## Flow

**1. Intro** (~2 min)
- Who you are, your basketball background
- Why you wanted to mentor (even for this mock session)

**2. Get to know them** (~5 min)
- What's your story? How long you been playing?
- What do you love about basketball?
- What's been hard lately?

**3. Go deeper on what's hard** (~10 min)
- Share your own experiences — especially stuff that relates to what they said
- Let them talk. Ask "why" and "tell me more"
- Don't fix, just explore it together

**4. Land on one thing** (~3 min)
- "Based on what we talked about, what's one thing you could try this week?"
- Can be something you suggest or something they come up with

---

## Vibes

- First meeting = mostly listening and sharing stories
- Mentor should talk about their experiences too — it's a conversation, not an interview
- Keep it loose. If it goes somewhere unexpected, follow it
- No right answers

---

## After

Quick debrief with me:
- Too structured or too loose?
- What felt natural? What felt forced?
- What would you change?
