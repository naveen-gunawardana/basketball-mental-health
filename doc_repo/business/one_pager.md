# Basketball Mentorship Project

## Problem
High school players grind but struggle with the mental side — unclear roles, no feedback on progress, confidence that disappears in games, fear of talking to coaches. Current options: generic therapy apps or "just work harder."

## Solution
**Weekly 15-min check-ins with college/former players who've been there.**

Not therapy. Not coaching. Just real talk — navigating playing time, building confidence, staying motivated through the grind.

*Think Big Brothers Big Sisters for basketball.*

## Status
- Interviewing SF high school players now
- Piloting with 2 UHS players
- Building mentor network from local coaches

## Ask
- **Want to mentor?** 15 min/week commitment
- **Know a player who'd benefit?** Intro them
- **Feedback?** Poke holes — what am I missing?
