**"How do you deal with it right now?"**
Prioritizes school first, then sports. Uses free time to work on whichever sport isn't in season.

**"What's frustrating about that?"**
Implied that finding time is the challenge - has to consciously make sure he's "playing enough sports to go after school."

**"How often does this come up?"**
- ☐ Daily ☒ Weekly ☐ Occasionally

**"What have you tried to fix it?"**
University and SFDA (middle school) prepared him well for balancing school, sports, and social life.

**"How does it affect you — time, stress, confidence, performance?"**
Said he feels he's "doing a good job" managing it. Not overly stressed - credits preparation from middle school.
