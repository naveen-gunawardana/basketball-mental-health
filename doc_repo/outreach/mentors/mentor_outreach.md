# Mentor Outreach

## Who We're Reaching
College players, former college players, and ex-pros who played basketball — especially those with ties to the SF Bay Area or local high school programs.

---

## Why Mentors Are Needed

High school players in SF are grinding through real problems — unclear roles, fading confidence, no one to talk to who actually gets it. Coaches are focused on winning. Parents mean well but haven't lived it. Therapists don't know basketball.

What these players need is someone who's been exactly where they are. Someone who navigated the bench, the coach conversation, the doubt spiral — and came out the other side. That's a college or former player. That's a mentor.

**One 45-minute conversation a week can change the trajectory of a player's season — or their relationship with the game entirely.**

---

## What We're Asking

- **Time:** ~45 min/week (or biweekly if needed)
- **Format:** Virtual 1:1 call — you pick the days and times
- **Commitment:** Flexible, low-pressure, built around your schedule

This is not coaching. Not therapy. Just real talk — the kind you wish you'd had.

---

## Why Mentors Say Yes

### Giving Back to the Game
Basketball gave you something. This is how you pass it forward. You don't need to have all the answers — you just need to have been there. Your story is the resource.

### Service Hours
Many college athletic programs require community service. Mentoring with Mentality Sports counts. We can provide documentation of hours for your program or resume.

### Resume + References
Mentorship experience reads well — whether you're going into coaching, sports management, business, or anything else. We'll support you with a letter of recommendation or reference if needed.

### You're Invited In
This isn't a form you fill out and disappear into. We want to know you. Mentors are invited to community conversations, get connected with other players in the network, and are part of building something real from the ground up.

---

## Mentor Profiles We're Looking For

- Currently playing at the college level (D1, D2, D3, NAIA, JUCO)
- Former college player
- Former pro (any level)
- Coaches or former coaches with playing experience

Basketball IQ matters. So does empathy. We'll match you with a player whose situation mirrors something you've actually lived.
