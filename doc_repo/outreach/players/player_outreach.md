# Player Outreach

## Who We're Reaching
High school basketball players in San Francisco, ages 13–18. Motivated to improve. Dealing with the mental side of the game — confidence, playing time, coach relationships, motivation.

---

## Core Message

You put in the work. You show up to practice. But something's still off — maybe it's confidence in games, unclear playing time, not knowing how to talk to your coach.

Mentality Sports pairs you with a college or former player who's been exactly where you are. Not a coach. Not a therapist. Someone who lived it and came out the other side.

**Weekly 1:1 calls with someone who gets it. Built around your schedule.**

---

## Outreach Channels

- Direct outreach to UHS players (pilot: Jet Black, Jacob Schulte)
- Coach referrals into high school programs
- SF Rec & Parks youth basketball leagues
- AAU teams in the Bay Area
- Word of mouth from pilot participants
- Social media (Instagram, TikTok — storytelling from mentor-athletes)

---

## What Players Get

- A mentor matched to their sport, position, and situation
- Weekly or biweekly 45-min virtual 1:1 sessions
- A space to talk about the stuff coaches don't have time for
- Goal setting and accountability
- Real talk on navigating playing time, coach conversations, and game confidence
