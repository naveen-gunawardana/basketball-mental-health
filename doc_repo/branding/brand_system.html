Text on Background Combinations

Primary surfaces · Nav · Footer

Navy + Parchment

Body copy reads cleanly on navy. Use for navigation, footers, and primary dark sections where authority and trust matter.

Default page · Cards · Content

Parchment + Navy

The everyday workhorse. Warm background with dark navy text — athletic without being sterile. Every page starts here.

CTA buttons · Alerts · Badges

Terracotta + White

High energy. Reserve for primary actions — "Get Started", "Find a Mentor", notification dots. Never use for large text blocks.

Wellness tags · Success · Progress

Sage + White

Calm and grounded. Use for mental health indicators, session complete states, wellness category labels.

Achievements · Milestones · Stars

Gold + Navy

For earned moments. Milestone badges, streak counts, rating stars. Feels like a trophy — use it sparingly so it lands.

Hero dark · Dashboard · Stats

Dark Navy + Sky

Deep and focused. Use for dark hero sections, data-heavy dashboard panels, and stat displays on dark backgrounds.

Soft cards · Profile panels · Forms

Blush + Charcoal

Warm and approachable. Use for secondary cards, form containers, and profile sections where parchment would blend too much.

Dark cards · Mentor profiles · Night

Charcoal + Mint Sage

Refined and grounded. Use for dark card surfaces, mentor profile cards in dark context, or any secondary dark surface.

Brand Rationale

Athletic Roots

Navy is the jersey. Orange is the ball. Parchment is the warmth of worn hardwood. Every color traces back to the court — grounded, not borrowed from a generic health app.

Mental Wellness

Sage brings calm without being clinical. It signals that this platform takes mental health seriously — without looking like a therapy app or a meditation product.

Achievement

Gold is reserved. When an athlete earns something — a streak, a milestone, a mentor rating — that moment needs weight. Use gold sparingly so it always lands.

Font Pairing Logic

Outfit — Display

Geometric and confident. Wide letter shapes that read large — built for headlines, hero copy, and names. It has personality without being loud.

Inter — Body

The workhorse. Neutral and highly legible at every size. Lets Outfit own the stage — Inter supports without competing. Feels modern and trustworthy.

Mono — Data

For numbers and facts. Stats, session counts, timestamps, hex codes. The tabular rhythm signals precision — athletes and their data deserve accuracy.
